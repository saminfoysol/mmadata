Which martial art is really most effective ?

an analysis of trends in the background of top mixed martial arts contenders

about

The sport of mixed martial arts allows an observable intersection between different martial arts. To determine which disciplines are most effective, we observed the backgrounds of current UFC champions and top 5 ranked fighters from every weight division. We only counted backgrounds where the fighter has high level experience. For example, for Jiu Jitsu this means a brown belt or higher, for wrestling, at least a top ranked state competitor in high school etc.
